How It Works

Simple steps to achieve complete transaction privacy

1

Create an Account

Sign up with a username and password. Your credentials are securely hashed and stored with modern encryption.

2

Make a Deposit

Deposit funds and receive a secret commitment. This creates an anonymous entry in our Merkle tree that only you can prove ownership of.

3

Wait for Mixing

As more users deposit, your transaction gets mixed with others, making it impossible to trace the source.

4

Withdraw Anonymously

Generate a Zero-Knowledge proof of your deposit and withdraw to any address without revealing your identity.
